A brief About DVEC

Dubai Virtual Exhibition Center is the first and largest virtual exhibition of its kind in the world in terms of space, participants and global competitiveness. Exhibition participants and visitors interact from anywhere in the world through the Livaatverse virtual reality platform, which is one of the latest smart interactive 3D technologies in the region. Dubai Virtual Exhibition Center offers all-in-one platform virtual event solutions that provides immersive 3D experience that simulates real life, as it allows participants to design virtual exhibitions and booths to display, market and sell products.

Virtual Conferences

Virtual conferences provides you, your partners, sponsors and attendees the ultimate platform to host webinars, network, share ideas and spark interesting discussions with people from around the world. Reach a greater audience by removing the need to travel, and see incredible results at a reduced cost to in-person conferences!.

Center Facilities

Virtual Auditorium

A true virtual auditorium experience where there is a stage, a backstage, and an audience watching the stage.

Virtual Exhibition Hall

Connect your exhibitors with buyers based anywhere in the world with an immersive virtual trade show. Exhibitors and attendees will enjoy Stunning, interactive and easily navigable virtual exhibit halls, Including Interactive Booths where exhibitors can showcase digital products and services through fully branded virtual stands.

Virtual Meeting Rooms

The virtual meeting rooms will foster business collaboration between attendees and exhibitors. Attendees and exhibitors can request and pre-schedule appointments with one another.
